Our Founding Story

From one father's prayer to a community's calling.

How SSA Began

SSA was born from the heart of Ishmael Sanders, a young father determined to raise his five sons in a spiritually grounded, value-driven environment. He prayed for a village. God sent one. From that village, the Southfield Sports Academy took life.

A Father's Vision Became a Community Mission

What began as one father's desire grew into a collective calling. A brotherhood formed. Today, SSA stands as a testament to faith, unity, mentorship, and intentional parenting.
